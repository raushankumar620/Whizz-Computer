About Us

Practical, Affordable, and Local

Whizz Computer is a Delhi-based training institute delivering hands-on IT and professional courses since 2016. We focus on practical learning — live projects, real-world tools, and small class sizes — so students finish ready for internships and jobs. Popular offerings include Web Development, Data Science, Cyber Security, Digital Marketing, Tally & Accounting, and Office Productivity. Our trainers are industry practitioners who mentor students through projects, interview prep, and portfolio reviews.
